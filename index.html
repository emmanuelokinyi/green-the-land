Our Mission

Through our initiative, we aim to raise awareness about climate change and its
devastating effects, while nurturing a sense of responsibility towards the planet in the younger generation.

At GreenTheLand, we're on a mission to empower high school graduates to make
a tangible impact on the environment by planting trees in primary schools.

Join us in creating a greener future for all!

FAQ

How can I get involved?
You can join our initiative by signing up on our website and attending our planting events.
Why plant trees in schools?
Planting trees in schools not only helps combat climate change but also educates children about environmental conservation.
Is there an age limit to participate?
Our initiative is open to high school graduates who are passionate about environmental sustainability.
What impact do trees have on the environment?
Trees play a crucial role in purifying the air, providing habitat for wildlife, and reducing carbon dioxide levels.
How can I support the cause financially?
You can donate to GreenTheLand to help fund tree planting initiatives and educational programs.

News Updates

Official Launch at Xaverian Primary, Kisumu

May 16, 2024

At last the idea was brought to existence.In a concerted effort to...

combat climate change, the initiative was officially launched at Xaverian Primary School. With a collective goal in mind, 500 trees were planted on the school premises. The event was not only about planting trees but also about raising awareness. Students, along with teachers, were engaged in insightful discussions highlighting the detrimental impacts of climate change. This initiative aimed to empower the younger generation with the knowledge and tools to become environmental stewards.

More trees planted at Victoria Primary, Kisumu

May 17, 2024

Continuing the momentum, the initiative embarked on a significant...

planting drive at Victoria Primary School. Over 1000 seedlings found their new home in the school's grounds, symbolizing a commitment to sustainability. Beyond the act of planting, students were educated on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), fostering a deeper understanding of global environmental challenges. This event served as a testament to the power of collective action in striving towards a greener, more sustainable future.

Planned Launch in Nairobi

June 15,2024

Join us for our planned launch in Nairobi.Looking ahead, the initiative eagerly...

anticipates its forthcoming launch in Nairobi. While the venue is yet to be confirmed, preparations are in full swing for the scheduled event on June 15th. With an open invitation extended to all, the initiative encourages individuals from all walks of life to join hands in this noble cause. Together, we can make a difference in safeguarding our planet for future generations. Join us as we take another step towards a more sustainable and resilient future.
